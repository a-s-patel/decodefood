What DecodeFood does

Scan any label

Advanced OCR reads ingredient lists directly from packaging — curved bottles, glossy bags, and everything in between.

Personalized diet profiles

Set your diet mode from 7+ options. Add specific allergens, intolerances, and custom flags. Your profile never leaves your device.

Science-based explanations

Every flagged ingredient comes with a plain-language breakdown: what it is, why it's there, and what it does to your body.

Privacy first

No account required. No data collected. Your dietary profile is stored locally and never transmitted to any server.

NOVA processing scale

Identifies ultra-processed foods using the scientific NOVA framework, with specific reasons — not arbitrary scores.

Barcode + OCR fallback

Scans barcode first for instant lookup. Falls back to direct OCR analysis when no database match exists.

Our philosophy

Data, Not Fear.

Most food apps give you a colored score and tell you what to think. We don't. DecodeFood is an objective translation tool — it converts chemical names and processing jargon into plain English so you can make your own informed decision. We don't label foods "good" or "bad." We don't push you toward buying alternatives. We give you the facts, and we trust you with them.
