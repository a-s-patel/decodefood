About DecodeFood

Built for the curious consumer.

Why We Built This

Food labels are designed to be confusing. Between complex chemical names, misleading marketing claims, and industrial additives with regulatory-approved aliases, it's nearly impossible for the average consumer to know what they're actually putting in their body.

We saw a gap between scientific research and consumer knowledge. Most apps in this space use proprietary algorithms to "score" food, which introduces bias and removes your agency. You end up trusting a colored circle instead of understanding the actual ingredient.

We wanted to build something fundamentally different — a tool that acts as a clinical translation layer between food chemistry and the kitchen table. Not a judge. Not a scorecard. A decoder.

The "Honest Advisor" Approach

Unlike other scanners that use "black-box" scoring systems to label foods as healthy or unhealthy, DecodeFood is an objective translation tool. Our Parallel Analysis Engine checks ingredients against your personal dietary profile — whether that involves life-threatening allergies, lifestyle diets like veganism, or a simple desire to avoid industrial additives.

The result isn't a score. It's a breakdown. We provide the science, the regulatory status, and the plain-English explanation for every flagged ingredient. Your data stays on your phone, the logic stays transparent, and the power stays with you.

How It Works

When you scan a label, DecodeFood performs a multi-layered Parallel Analysis in real time:

01

Capture

The camera reads the ingredient list via on-device OCR, or scans a barcode to retrieve data from the Open Food Facts database. You can also type ingredients manually.

02

Analyze

The Parallel Pipeline checks every ingredient simultaneously across three layers: your personal allergy & intolerance profile, your dietary mode (Vegan, Vegetarian, Pescatarian, etc.), and the NOVA ultra-processing scale.

03

Explain

Flagged ingredients surface with a plain-language explanation: what it is, why it's in the product, and what it does in the body. Science-based, not clickbait.

04

Decide

You get the facts. The decision is always yours. DecodeFood never tells you what to eat or ranks foods against each other.

What We Don't Do

✗ Assign arbitrary health scores or ratings
✗ Tell you a food is "good" or "bad"
✗ Recommend alternative products to buy
✗ Collect, store, or transmit your dietary profile
✗ Require an account or login
✗ Use advertising or sponsored results
